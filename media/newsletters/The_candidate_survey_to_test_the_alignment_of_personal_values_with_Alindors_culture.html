Cultural fit evaluation

As part of the selection process for our AI high-tech startup, we are conducting an evaluation focused on soft skills and cultural fit. This step is critical in identifying candidates who not only meet the technical requirements but also align with our company's work culture and values.

Our aim is to assess your compatibility with the team's dynamics, your approach to problem-solving, collaboration, and adaptability in a rapidly evolving environment. This evaluation is designed to gauge how well you might integrate and contribute to our projects and overall mission.

There are no bad answers, as well as no bad candidates or employees. You can be not good for our startup, but perfect for another company. And vice versa. So don't hesitate to be honest and direct.

Interesting fact! Candidates often strive for perfection when completing forms like this, presenting themselves in the best possible light. However, during interviews, it becomes apparent—either directly or indirectly. It's likely that the hiring manager will revisit specific responses during your interview to understand your answers more deeply. This is an opportunity to ensure your responses genuinely represent your approach and abilities, as accuracy and honesty are valued in this process.
